Champagne

Red Wine

  • Bordeaux - Château Lynch-Bages
  • With its rich black fruit flavors, hints of tobacco, and firm tannins, this wine pairs beautifully with entrees like Beef Wellington, enhancing the richness and depth of the dish.
  • California - Silver Oak Cabernet Sauvignon
  • With notes of blackberry, cassis, and vanilla, this wine complements the tender flavors of all our Entree options.

White Wine

  • Burgundy - Louis Jadot Puligny-Montrachet
  • A premier white wine from Burgundy, Louis Jadot Puligny-Montrachet offers elegance and finesse with its delicate aromas of citrus, pear, and hazelnut, balanced by crisp acidity and a creamy texture
  • German - Dr. Loosen Riesling
  • Its off-dry style and nuanced sweetness make it a versatile pairing for a variety of our dishes, including our appetizer options like Prawn Cocktail and Smoked Salmon Canapés, enhancing their delicate flavors with a touch of sweetness.

Non-Alcoholic Option

  • Sparkling Elderflower Lemonade
  • Its effervescence and subtle sweetness enhance the flavors of the seafood while refreshing the palate between bites. Additionally, its sophisticated presentation makes it an excellent choice for those seeking a non-alcoholic option that still offers a touch of elegance to the dining experience.
